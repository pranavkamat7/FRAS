What is Face Recognition Attendance System?
A face recognition attendance system is a tool that uses facial recognition technology to identify a person using his/her facial features and automatically marks the presence of the person. Face recognition attendance is a contactless way to manage students in an organization. Unlike other biometric attendance management systems such as fingerprints that capture a human identity through a touch, a face recognition attendance tool is a touchless way to manage student attendance.
